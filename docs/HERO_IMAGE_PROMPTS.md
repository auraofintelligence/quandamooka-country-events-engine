# Hero Image Prompts

All images were generated as public-safe, non-documentary website assets. They intentionally avoid readable text, logos, flags, sacred cultural symbols and identifiable close-up people.

## hero-engine.webp

Cinematic, respectful public website hero for an event-planning engine: dusk coastal planning table, abstract island map, ferry routes, venue markers, schedules, tablets, safety notes, small model marquees, bushland textures and beach sand samples. Deep ink, aqua, gold, coral and violet accents.

## hero-calendar.webp

Dusk beach and bushland edge with a calm, well-managed festival layout: low stages, market tents, small crowd clusters from a distance, ferry lights, volunteer wayfinding, waste stations and safety lighting.

## hero-ecosystem.webp

Illuminated wall map and table of public event ecosystem categories: venues, parks, beaches, catering, artists, planners, gear, transport and noticeboards. Abstract connectors and no readable labels.

## hero-builders.webp

Laptop, tablet and printed checklist on a coastal workbench. Abstract form fields and Markdown blocks as glowing shapes with no readable words, surrounded by venue cards, approval marks, transport tokens and gear icons.

## hero-simulation.webp

Island event simulation control room: glowing digital twin table with crowd-flow ribbons, ferry arcs, waste and water gauges, tide/weather layers, approval gates and safety indicators without words.
